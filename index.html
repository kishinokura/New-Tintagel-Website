Two Parallel Class Paths

Students choose either Kishido or Bushido as their primary class path. Both paths follow the same rank structure, and all students compete together in tournaments and Challenge Days.

Kishido

A knightly path centered on technical discipline, composure, and progressive combat skill.

Bushido

A samurai path emphasizing commitment, form, and powerful tactical application.

Rank progression is equivalent across both paths. Kishido and Bushido students train for the same tournament events and Challenge Days.
